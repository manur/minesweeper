Objective

So, you've mastered Minesweeper. And you can mark mines like a boss. Reverse minesweeper has the same objective. To mark all mines.

Rules

  1. Some of the blank squares are mines.
  2. Clicking on a blank square will mark it as a mine.
  3. Clicking again will unmark it as a mine.
  4. When you mark all mines, and no incorrect boxes, you win.
  5. You cannot lose, except by giving up.
